RANDOM RECIPE

  1. Slice and dice some green and red peppers.
  2. Dump the peppers in a frying pan with butter for 5 minutes.
  3. Slice and dice some chicken breast into cube pieces.
  4. Dump the chicken into a frying pan for 10 minutes.
  5. Halfway through cooking the chicken dump 1/2 cup of teryaki sauce.
  6. Serve with rice and you have chicken teryaki.
