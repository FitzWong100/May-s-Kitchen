Who am I?

by May Kitty

Oh hello there, I just happen to be a happy baker living in the big apple.

I love baking with the deepest passion. Ever since I was a little female I wanted to be a baker. However, I was unable to fulfil my dream because of bills and responsibilities. Nevertheless, I have decided to risk it all and become a baker. I have learned from the best, and I am now one of the best bakery chefs in the tristate area.
